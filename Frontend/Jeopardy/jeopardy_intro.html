Music Jeopardy

How to Play

1

Select a category and point value (e.g., "2010s for 300")

2

A clue will be revealed - respond in the form of a question

3

You have 10 seconds to answer each question

Scoring

  • Correct answer: Gain points equal to the clue value
  • Incorrect answer: Lose points equal to the clue value

Tips

  • Always phrase answers as questions (e.g., "What is Bohemian Rhapsody?")
  • Higher point values mean more difficult questions
  • Don't wait too long - the 10-second timer starts immediately!
